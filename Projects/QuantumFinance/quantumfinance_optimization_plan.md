# QuantumFinance Project Optimization Plan

## Overview
QuantumFinance is a revolutionary portfolio optimization and risk analysis framework that leverages quantum computing algorithms to provide exponential speedups over classical financial modeling techniques. The project implements Variational Quantum Eigensolver (VQE) and other quantum algorithms for portfolio optimization, demonstrating practical quantum advantage in financial applications. This optimization plan identifies 50 specific tasks to enhance quantum algorithms, financial modeling accuracy, risk analysis capabilities, and real-world investment applications.

## Architecture Analysis
- **Technology Stack**: Swift, quantum algorithms (VQE, QAOA), financial mathematics, AI integration
- **Core Features**: Quantum portfolio optimization, risk analysis, VQE implementation, quantum supremacy demonstration
- **Quantum Methods**: VQE, Quantum Amplitude Estimation, Quantum Search algorithms
- **Financial Models**: Modern Portfolio Theory, risk metrics, constraint optimization
- **Current Status**: Functional quantum finance engine with portfolio optimization and risk analysis capabilities
